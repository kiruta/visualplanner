Planning sucks!



A good organization is a key factor for success and planning is the most important part of any activity.


Traditional planning tools are often difficult to read or lack the ability to manage complex tasks.


Gantt Charts are often difficult to follow: aligning elements with the timeline, resources and the workload sometimes takes most of the working time.


A whole new approach



We built a new tool that combines the abilities to manage complex tasks of traditional Gantt charts with the immediate visual response of Mind Maps.



Planto is a Gantt Map, a powerful hybrid that scrolls vertically and kills all the clustering of traditional planning.

  • Effectiveness of Gantt chart and simplicity of Mind map at the same time.
  • Milestones, linked to the timeline - no more missed deadlines
  • Easy links suport and management
  • Visible plan executions status
  • Smooth Vertical scrolling
  • Simply works...for you!
