Charts and maps


Being heavilly adopted on every level of planning, they still fail to bring both siplicity and effectiveness at the same time. Most of the project management and planning management tolls require great amount of time spend on maintaining.


Gantt chart at one hand provide ability to manage complex relation and plan lots of aspect, buy they are totally useless for non-experienced users and teams.


Another great tool - Mind map provides ability to eassily crate roadmaps, milestones and time. At the same time it lacks time control, that other tolls have.


Therefore we desided to create Planto

  • Solution of a better Better Gantt chart with an effectiveness and simplicity of the Mind map
  • Vertical workflow design
  • Milestones, linked to the timeline, to control due dates and flow
  • Easy links suport and management
  • Shows plan executions status with a first look
  • Simply works
